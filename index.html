ABOUT MCC

Magizh Clinical Care (MCC) was established in 1997 under the visionary guidance of SV Trust, a renowned non-profit organization committed to public welfare and healthcare excellence. Since its inception, SV Trust has aimed to bridge the gap between advanced medical treatment and accessibility for every individual, especially those in underprivileged communities. MCC stands as a symbol of this mission, operating as a state-of-the-art, multi-specialty hospital that provides comprehensive healthcare services. The hospital is known for its skilled medical professionals, modern infrastructure, and patient-centric approach. With 24/7 emergency care, specialized departments, and high-end diagnostic facilities, MCC ensures quality treatment and timely intervention. In addition to clinical services, SV Trust also actively organizes health camps, awareness drives, and free consultation programs as part of its social responsibility. Both MCC and SV Trust work hand-in-hand to uphold values of compassion, integrity, and innovation in healthcare delivery, making them a trusted name in the community for over two decades.
